Reading and Activities

The Self Reliance Services (SRS) “Find a Better Job” manual is intended for use in a group, but you will use it individually. Activities and discussions in the reading should be done individually.

This week, you should complete the following reading:

  • Chapter 4, Introduction, Page 61

  • Chapter 4, Section 1—Make Powerful Impressions, Pages 61–63

    • In the activity, do the role playing on your own.

  • Chapter 4, Section 2—Follow the Power Statement Formula, Pages 63–66 (excluding the second activity)

    • In the first activity, share your power statements with two or more friends or family members and ask for feedback.

  • Chapter 4, Conclusion, Page 67

    • You should watch the video The Power of Power Statements referenced in this section.

Assignment

During the reading from the SRS manual “Finding a Better Job,” you completed an activity where you wrote some power statements and shared it with others to obtain feedback. Your assignment this week is to finalize two strong power statements that you can share with a potential employer.

  1. Download the cse310_power_statements.docx template.
