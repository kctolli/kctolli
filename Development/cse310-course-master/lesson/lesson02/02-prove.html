W02 Prove: Practice Job Networking

Reading and Activities

The “Finding a Job” readings in this course will all come from the Self Reliance Services (SRS) “Find a Better Job” manual published by the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. You should download this manual for use in this course. You will complete a reading and a Prove assignment related to employment every other week.

The SRS manual is intended for use in a group, but you will use it individually. Activities and discussions in the reading should be done individually.

This week, you should complete the following reading:

  • Chapter 2, Introduction, Page 25

  • Chapter 2, Section 1—How People Really Get Jobs, Page 26

    • You should watch the video How Jobs Are Really Filled referenced in this section.

    • In the activity, after you make the list of potential contacts within 60 seconds, share with a friend or family member and ask for some sources or specific names you may have missed.

  • Chapter 2, Section 2—Talk to Everyone, Pages 27–29

Additionally, spend a few minutes looking at the networking resources at the BYU-Idaho Career Center.

Assignment

During the reading from the SRS manual “Find a Better Job,” you completed an activity where you made a list of potential contacts you could network with to find employment opportunities. Your assignment this week is to expand this list to 20 potential contacts.

  1. Download the cse310_networking.docx template.

  2. Starting with the list you created during the reading activity, create a list of 20 potential contacts you could network with to find employment opportunities. For each contact, include a description of who they are (for example: former co-worker, teacher, former youth leader, relative, friend already employed).
