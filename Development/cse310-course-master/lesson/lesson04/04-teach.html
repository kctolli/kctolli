W04 Teach: Create Team Project Plan

Team Meeting Agenda

You need to meet together as a team at least once this week. Your recommended agenda is as follows:

  1. Opening prayer.

  2. Select a member of the team to be the project team leader.

  3. Agree on a proposed project and complete the Project Plan (see below) together.

  4. Identify what needs to be researched and make assignments to team members. It is recommended that team members write code (proof of concept) to demonstrate the results of their research.

You may need to refer back to the Team Project Description.

Team Project Plan

  1. Download cse310_project_plan.docx.

  2. Fill all parts of the project plan include proposal, schedule, and identification of the project team leader.

You may start work on your project after submitting your plan. However, you may be asked by your teacher to modify your proposed project. You should respond to feedback from your teacher quickly to get an approved project plan.
