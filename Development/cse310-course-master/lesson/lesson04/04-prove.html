W04 Prove: Practice Me in 30 Seconds

Reading and Activities

The Self Reliance Services (SRS) “Find a Better Job” manual is intended for use in a group, but you will use it individually. Activities and discussions in the reading should be done individually.

This week, you should complete the following reading:

  • Chapter 2, Section 3—Me in 30 Seconds, Page 29–31 (excluding the last activity)

    • In the first activity, ask a friend or family member to time your “me in 30 seconds.”

    • In the second activity, share your “me in 30 seconds” with two or more friends or family members and ask for feedback.

  • Chapter 2, Conclusion, Page 32

    • You should watch the video Basketball Got Me a Job referenced in this section.

Assignment

During the reading from the SRS “Finding a Better Job” manual, you completed an activity where you wrote a “Me in 30 Seconds” and shared it with others to obtain feedback. Your assignment this week is to update your "Me in 30 Seconds" and practice it with a friend or family member.

  1. Download the cse310_me_in_30seconds.docx template.

  2. Write your updated “Me in 30 Seconds” based on the one you completed in the reading activity.

  3. Provide the name of the person that you practiced the “Me in 30 Seconds” with.
