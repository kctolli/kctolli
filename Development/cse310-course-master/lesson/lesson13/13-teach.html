W13 Teach: Create Team Project Report

Team Meeting Agenda

You need to meet at least once together as a team this week. Your recommended agenda should be as follows:

  1. Opening prayer.

  2. Each team member should report on their assignments.

  3. Follow up on assignments for team members to complete the project. Ensure that the README.md file is completed.

  4. Recommend team members use the GitHub fork option to create their own copy of the project for future use.

  5. Complete the Team Project Report (see below) as a team.

You may need to refer back to the Team Project Description.

Team Project Report

  1. Download cse310_team_project_report.docx.

  2. Complete the Team Project report together including your report on the completion of requirements from the Project Plan.
