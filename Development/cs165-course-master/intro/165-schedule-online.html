CS 165 Schedule

This course is organized at the week level. For assignments and due dates, there will be a consistent pattern every week.

A few things to note:

  • This schedule is subject to change at the instructor's discretion.
  • This course has been designed for the average student to earn an average grade by working 3 hours per credit hour (or 9 hours per week) (as indicated by the approximate times shown below). Individual circumstances may require more/less time to be invested.

Approximate weekly time investment:

  • Reading - 1 hour
  • Checkpoint A - 45 minutes
  • Checkpoint B - 45 minutes
  • Team Activity - 1 hour
  • Assignment/Project - 5.5 hours
  • Developer's Forum - Integrated through the work and time of each item listed above

Due Dates

All items due at 11:00pm (US Mountain Time) on the date noted
