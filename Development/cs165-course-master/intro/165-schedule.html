CS 165 Schedule

This course is organized at the week level. The following table shows the plan for how we will try to use class time, the suggested plan for what students should be working on outside of class, and when items are due.

A few things to note:

  • Items are due at 11:59pm on the days noted.
  • This schedule is subject to change, but is the ideal we will shoot for.
  • This course has been designed for the average student to earn an average grade by working 2 hours outside of class for every hour inside of class (as indicated by the approximate times shown in this table). Individual circumstances may require more/less time to be invested.
