CS 165 F2F Course Setup Notes

This document contains a list of the steps to convert the online course to a Face to Face course.

Each semester, the course improvement department will change the due dates, etc for the next semester. Then, we can copy the reference course to be a F2F reference, make a few changes there, and then let each professor create their F2F section from that source.

  1. Copy the course to a F2F Reference section.
  2. Remove the following:
    1. Welcome - Syllabus
    2. Welcome - Schedule
    3. Welcome - Team Activities
    4. Welcome - Developers' Forum
    5. Welcome - Developers' Forum Instructions
    6. Welcome - The complete "Other Online Learning Help" sub module
    7. Week 01 - Ice Breaker
    8. Week 01 - Availability Survey
    9. Week 01 - Syllabus Quiz
    10. Week 06 - Mid Course Evaluation
    11. Week 13 - End of Course Evaluation
    12. Every Week - The Practice Problems
  3. Add the following (From the F2F files sub-module):
    1. Welcome - Syllabus
    2. Welcome - Schedule
  4. Remove from the quizzes:
    1. Simulation Quiz
    2. All of the practice problems quizzes (This might happen automatically from deleting them in the TOC.)
  5. Remove from the Discussions:
    1. Ice Breaker
    2. Simulation Discussion
    3. Then, un-attach each of the developer forums with the gradebook.
  6. Remove from the gradebook:
    1. Syllabus Quiz
    2. Mid course feedback
    3. Developer's forum assignments
  7. Add to the gradebook:
    1. Exams category worth 15% of the final grade.
    2. 14 Teach : Team Final Exam Review - In the Team Activities category, worth: 160 points
    3. 14 Prove : Final Exam - In a the new exams category, worth 40 points, 100% of that category.
  8. Copy in the Final Exam:
    1. Copy the Final Exam from a previous semester.
    2. Set a resonable password for it.
    3. Connect it to the gradebook item.
    4. Add it to the content.
    5. Give it a due date.
  9. Other things to think about:
    1. The instructor info on the "overview" landing page.
