App Submission

This week is the final app submission. By the end of this week, all loose ends should be tied up, and you should have an app that meets the core requirements you outlined with your sponsor.

One member of your team should generate the final APK for the app and upload it to I-Learn. To do so using Android Studio, click the Build menu, and select "Build APK".

Make sure you also schedule time to provide the final APK to your sponsor.

Submission

Submit one APK per team.
